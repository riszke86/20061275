FaQ

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What are the opening hours?
WildHeart Eco Park is open every day from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, including weekends and most public holidays.

2. Can I buy tickets online?
Yes. Visitors can purchase tickets through our website or at the entrance on the day of their visit.

3. Are guided tours available?
Yes. Guided tours are available throughout the day and provide interesting facts about our animals, habitats, and conservation programmes.

4. Is the park suitable for children?
Absolutely! WildHeart Eco Park offers family-friendly exhibits, educational activities, and interactive experiences suitable for visitors of all ages.

5. Can I feed the animals?
Only during supervised feeding sessions with our trained staff. This helps keep both visitors and animals safe.

6. Is parking available?
Yes. Free parking is available for all visitors, including accessible parking spaces near the main entrance.

7. Are food and drinks available?
Yes. Our café serves a variety of meals, snacks, and drinks throughout the day.
